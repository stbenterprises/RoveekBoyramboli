The Story

Nobody showed me the way. No mentor. No inheritance.
No blueprint. Just a boy from Mauritius —
and a vision too big to ignore.

So I started. Alone. From scratch. There were days when the vision was so big and the resources so small that stopping would have made sense. Days when no one understood what I was building or why. Days when the only person who believed in it was me.

I kept going. Not for pride. Not for recognition. For something far deeper than that.

For my mother, Suydin — who gave me a home when the world offered me nothing. The first pillar of my company bears her initial. The city I am building carries her name.

For Mauritius — this small, extraordinary island that made me everything I am. I refuse to believe that where you are born determines how far you can go. I am living proof that it does not.

For every person who was ever told their dreams were too big for their circumstances.
